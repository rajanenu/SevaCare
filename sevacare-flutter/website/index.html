Not a hospital. An ERP platform.

One system, every role, one source of truth.

Hospitals don't run on a single app today — they run on registers, WhatsApp groups, phone calls and a dozen disconnected tools. SevaCare replaces that sprawl with a single multi-tenant platform: every hospital gets its own secure, branded instance, while every person involved in care — from the admin who onboards the hospital to the patient booking a token — works inside the same connected system.

🏥

Multi-tenant by design

Each hospital or clinic gets an isolated schema, its own branding, and its own admins — provisioned in minutes, not months.

🔗

Connected, not siloed

Bookings, prescriptions, queues, and reviews flow between every role automatically — no manual re-entry, no lost paperwork.

📈

Built to extend

Today: booking, queues, records. Tomorrow: pharmacy dispensing and insurance claims — on the same foundation.

What's already built

Real features, running today.

🎟️

Slot + Token booking

Patients choose a fixed time slot or an unlimited queue-order token — whichever the doctor supports.

📡

Live queue & wait alerts

Patients see their real position and get an "almost your turn" alert — no more waiting-room guesswork.

⭐

Doctor ratings & reviews

Verified, post-consultation 5-star reviews build trust before a patient ever books.

📋

Digital prescriptions

Every prescription and medical history record lives with the patient, searchable, forever.

📍

Nearby & pincode search

Detect location once, and see hospitals in your own pincode surface first.

🔳

QR-code walk-in booking

Walk-in patients scan a hospital QR code to join the queue without a front-desk detour.

📊

Admin analytics

Channel mix, staff performance, and revenue — in one dashboard, updated live.

🔐

Secure by tenant

Every hospital's data is isolated at the schema level — no cross-tenant leakage, ever.
